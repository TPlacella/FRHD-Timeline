FRHD Timeline - 2013

October
18/10 Free Rider HD Facebook and Twitter pages created
26/10 Kano Apps YouTube Channel created
November
05/11 Free Rider HD is created, both game and forums
05/11 First Forum Member is kanoadmin and more Administrators/Moderators join: jschnare and Char (Game Developer)
11/12 FRHD is linked in WebGames Community on Reddit, spreading the word of the game's creation
14/11 Velksy, another Administrator/Moderator, joins Forums
19/11 Community Classic Tracks posted. Wild West by weewam is the first track on the Free Rider HD website, starting from track ID 1001. Tracks are imported from Canvas Rider and posted on developer-made accounts.
21-22/11 More Kano members and admins join forums: Eric, mi7ch, Ira (aka Ira Willey, creator of OneMoreLevel.com), timteh, Griffin, Edmund and kano_admin (pixelsplitter).
22/11 The first forum thread and message is made in "What is your favourite track?" by Ira
24/11 Char posts the first Forum/Game Update about how to get the best performance on FRHD
28/11 Char posts an update on how to Register to Forums before the game and forums are opened to the public
December
01/12 Experience FRHD like it was before the official release. You can play Epic NthShore by moose_man captured on the Wayback Machine
04/12 Free Rider HD Forums is now open to the public, WheelieMaker (weem) is the first normal member on forums (member #17). niclauzon makes the first post and first forum thread by a normal member.
05/12 Free Rider HD is officially released
05/12 Eric Welcomes the Community whilst talking about the current state of the game upon its release and future updates
08/12 Anything and Everything Not Free Rider topic forum added by Eric after pssst suggested it
11/12 Notifications released in-game for when your time is beaten on a track leaderboard
13/12 Chrome Web App for FRHD is released (only available on ChromeOS)
15/12 Post Your WIP Screenshot Here started by Coffee
16/12 Avatar and Sign-up methods released relating to Gravatar, Google+, Facebook and regular emails
18/12 Race/Ghost Links are now functional
20/12 IRL Pictures thread started by Hashplant
