--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- 19/11 Community Classic Tracks posted. Wild West by weewam is the first track on the Free Rider HD website, starting from track ID 1001. Tracks are imported from Canvas Rider and posted on developer-made accounts of those authors. Each track is titled '[TRACK NAME] is a Free Rider Community Classic Track by [AUTHOR]' in the description. Community Classic Tracks are contained within track IDs 1001 and 11095, meaning 10,095 Community Classic Tracks were posted. Players could ask on forums to gain access to these developer-made accounts with all their old tracks upon public release.
